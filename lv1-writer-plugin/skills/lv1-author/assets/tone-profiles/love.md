# Tone profile — love

- **Genre:** love / relationships (reflective non-fiction; the author's own story and view)
- **Register:** warm, intimate, reflective
- **Proof apparatus:** none–low — the user's experience leads; any factual claim (psychology of relationships, statistics) is cited and graded
- **Default authorship:** **interview-driven** — the user's relationships and reflections are the spine
- **Structure shape:** scene/letter → reflection → insight, per chapter
- **Density & pacing:** **expand** — linger in the remembered moment; let the feeling land before the reflection. Expansion is rendered experience, never filler

> An honest, intimate book about love grounded in the user's real experience. AI
> elicits and renders; it does not invent the relationships or the feelings. Read
> order: Requirements → Whose truth → Tone → Structure → Review. **If two rules
> conflict, the user's lived truth wins.**

---

## Whose truth

- **The relationships, moments, and reflections come from the user**, gathered via
  `references/interview.md` into `manuscript/intake.md`. AI invents no scene, no person,
  no exchange, no feeling. The account is true-as-lived; not graded.
- A skip is a **declared gap** — ask in a batched round or keep the chapter leaner;
  never fabricate intimacy the user didn't describe.
- Any **external factual claim** (relationship psychology, a study, a statistic) is
  cited and graded, and scoped honestly — no pop-psych over-reach.
- Handle privacy with care: only render what the user is willing to share about others;
  flag where names/details should be changed at the user's request.

## Tone & voice

- Warm, reflective, intimate — the user's own voice and emotional register.
- Honest about ambivalence and difficulty; resist the tidy romantic cliché.
- Tender, not sentimental; specific, not greeting-card.
- **Read `references/anti-tells.md`.** The therapy-speak register (`hold space`, `your
  journey`, `lean into`, `give yourself grace`…) is an automatic FIX-IT here — write the
  user's actual voice, not a wellness-post template.

## Structure & format

- Scene or remembered moment → reflection → the insight it yields; chronological or
  thematic by the user's arc.
- **Format follows content** (see `references/draft.md`): **prose** almost throughout —
  this is felt, not decoded. No tables/diagrams; markup only if the user is itemizing.

## Process

1. Interview for the relationships, moments, and reflections, and the privacy
   boundaries.
2. Draft scene by scene from `intake.md`; batched round when a moment needs detail only
   the user has.
3. Verify every detail traces to the user's account; check any external fact; confirm
   privacy choices.

## Never do this

- Invent a relationship, a scene, a line, or a feeling the user didn't share.
- Flatten real ambivalence into a clean romantic arc.
- Over-claim relationship "science"; dress an opinion as a finding.
- Expose private detail about another person beyond what the user agreed to share.
