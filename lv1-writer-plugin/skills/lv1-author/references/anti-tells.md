# Anti-tells — the AI register to write *out* of

Read this before drafting any profile; the inspector checks against it (tone conformance).
These are the words and constructions that mark prose as machine-generated. They are not
banned from the dictionary — most have an honest literal use. They are banned as
**reflexive filler**: the word the model reaches for when it isn't saying anything.

The rule: **a literal, earned use is fine; a reflexive, decorative one is a FIX-IT.** "She
navigated the canal in a narrowboat" is fine. "Navigating the complexities of modern love"
is a tell. When in doubt, cut the word and see if the sentence lost any meaning — if not, it
was a tell.

## Generic-AI tells

`delve`, `tapestry`, `testament` (as in "a testament to"), `navigate the complexities`,
`it's worth noting that`, `it's important to note`, `in today's fast-paced world`, `in the
realm of`, `landscape` (figurative — "the funding landscape"), `supercharge`, `unlock` (your
potential / insights), `beacon`, `realm`, `embark on`, `at the end of the day`, `when it
comes to`, `the world of`, `a rich tapestry of`, `ever-evolving`, `multifaceted`,
`underscores`, `boasts` (a feature).

## Therapy-speak tells (warm/intimate profiles especially)

`hold space`, `lean into` / `leaning in`, `sit with` (your feelings), `your journey`, `the
journey`, `do the work`, `give yourself grace`, `show up for yourself`, `honor your
truth`, `inner child`, `hold the tension`, `meet yourself where you are`, `it's okay to
not be okay`, `radical self-acceptance`, `pour into your cup`, `boundaries are an act of
self-love`. Exception: if the *user's own material* uses one of these phrases, it's theirs
to keep — render their voice, don't sand it off.

## Hype tells (mindset / persuasive / marketing drift)

`game-changer`, `revolutionary`, `seamless`, `cutting-edge`, `next-level`, `transformative`
(unless you show the transformation), `unprecedented` (unless it literally is and you say
why), `paradigm shift`, `this will change your life`.

## Structural tells

- **The empty pivot:** "But here's the thing…", "Here's the kicker…", "And that's the
  beauty of it." Earn the turn with content, don't announce it.
- **The triad reflex:** forcing every idea into a rule-of-three list ("clearer, faster,
  stronger") when the content has two points or four.
- **The summary echo:** restating the paragraph you just wrote as its own closing sentence.
- **Throat-clearing openers:** "In order to understand X, we must first…", "Let's dive in."

## How the inspector applies this

Tone conformance (`agents/lv1-inspect.md`, check 6) flags **a cluster of tells**, or **any
therapy-speak in a warm-profile draft**, as a FIX-IT — not a single incidental literal use.
The target is the *register*, not one word. A draft that reads like a LinkedIn wellness post
fails even if no single phrase is individually damning.
